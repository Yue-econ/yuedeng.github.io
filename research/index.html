Research

Job Market Paper

  • “Secondary Markets and the Opioid Crisis: Evidence from a Laboratory Experiment ” with Daniel Houser
    [abstract]

We study the effect of shutting down the secondary market on physicians' prescription behavior using a equilibrium model developed by Schnell and provide empirical knowledge through an lab experiment. The experiment isolates the effect on the physician-subjects' prescription behavior by controlling the anonymity of subjects and the availability of the secondary market.
